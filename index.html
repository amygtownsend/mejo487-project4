Franklin Street Restaurant Changes

Franklin Street is home to over 100 restaurants frequented often by Chapel Hill and Carrboro dwellers as well as UNC students. The collection of restaurants represents a wide range of cuisines, including American, Mexican, Chinese and Indian food.

The most popular food to get on Franklin St. are burgers and pizza with 12 burger joints and 11 pizza places to choose from.
