Franklin Street Restaurants

Franklin Street is home to over 100 restaurants frequented often by Chapel Hill and Carrboro dwellers as well as UNC students. The collection of restaurants represents a wide range of cuisines, including American, Mexican, Chinese and Indian food. The most popular food to get on Franklin St. are burgers and pizza with 12 burger joints and 11 pizza places to choose from.

According to the Downtown Chapel Hill Partnership, the number of open businesses on Franklin Street from January 2013 to January 2018 had a retention rate of 75.6 percent for the 5-year period. This means that one-fourth of the restaurants on Franklin St. closed in just five years.

There are two restaurants on Franklin Street that are significantly older than any other restaurants: Carolina Coffee Shop and Sutton's Drug Store. Carolina Coffee Shop was founded in 1922 and Sutton's opened shortly afterwards in 1923. Apart from these two, the other restaurants only go back as far as the 1970's, with Ye Olde Waffle Shop, which opened in 1973, as the next oldest restaurant.
